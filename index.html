It's a game that I made that pits eight different meme characters against each other. You can find it under "Games" on the top menu. If I was asked what my life's work was, I'd probably say that.

Why do you use Scratch?


Meme Clash started as a Scratch game, and I didn't want to convert it since I was used to the interface. Technically, now it's a javascript application.
